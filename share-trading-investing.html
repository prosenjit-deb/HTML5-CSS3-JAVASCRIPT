Share Trading Education

Helen Tarrant is a successful Commercial Property Investor who has along with her husband built a Commercial Property portfolio of over $10 Million dollars within 5 years.

Helen holds a Bachelors in Laws and a Masters in Laws degree from Australian National University along with her Real Estate Licence. Helen realised very quickly that one of the main inhibitors to Commercial Real Estate investment is lack of understanding how it works and where to find those properties. Therefore, in 2016 Helen started to teach Commercial Property Investment through seminars all around Australia. Today she has over 500 students join her program each year from around Australia to learn the ins and outs of Commercial Property investment.

Helen migrated to Australia at the age of 7 with her mother who only had $70 on her. From a migrant child who cannot speak English to working as a beauty therapist to finally gaining financial freedom through Commercial Property… Helen will take you on an amazing journey where she will show you how you too can obtain financial freedom through understanding Commercial Property and the different strategies behind it to accelerate your property portfolio while obtaining massive cash flow. Helen is passionate about changing people’s lives through Commercial Property Investment and has helped numerous students in finding the right Commercial Property with both security and confidence.
